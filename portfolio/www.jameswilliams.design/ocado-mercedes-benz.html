OCADO / MERCEDES

I worked on an internal e-commerce application designed to streamline parts transactions between Ocado and Mercedes-Benz.

The aim was to to enhance the process for Ocado's van depots.

The outcome was a system that enabled Ocado engineers to swiftly and accurately acquire the parts they needed. Improving workflow and boosting sales, with the platform facilitating transactions exceeding £500k / year.
